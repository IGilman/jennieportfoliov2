Jennie Gilman

With over five years experience in media and entertainment marketing, Jennie is a professional marketer currently working at Condé Nast on the Travel team driving revenue across brands such as Bon Appétit, Condé Nast Traveler, Vogue, The New Yorker and more for advertisers such as Tourism Ireland, Charleston CVB, Collier County Tourism Board and Silversea Cruises. Previously, she worked on the Integrated Marketing team at Warner Media for TBS and TNT networks helping to secure new revenue across advertisers such as Boston Beer, eBay, Denny's, Universal Orlando and more while managing the day to day added value and content partnerships for a number of agencies and holding companies such as Horizon, Omnicom Media Group and Publicis. She has a proven track record in brand partnerships, sales relationships and successfully executed campaigns and is a driven, enthusiastic and hard-wording team member.

Outside of her professional roles, Jennie can be found running in Central Park, trying new recipes (especially with her new sourdough starter), and traveling around the world.
